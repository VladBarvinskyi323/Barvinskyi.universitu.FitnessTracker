What is a good beginner workout plan?

A good beginner workout plan typically includes a mix of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility exercises. Start with light to moderate intensity and gradually increase the intensity and duration as your fitness improves.

Back

How often should I work out?

It's generally recommended to get at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week, along with strength training exercises at least twice a week. However, listen to your body and adjust as needed.

Back

What should I eat before and after a workout?

Before a workout, eat a small meal or snack that includes carbohydrates and protein about 1-2 hours before. After a workout, refuel with a meal that includes protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats within 2 hours to help repair and rebuild muscles.

Back

How can I prevent injuries?

To prevent injuries, warm up before exercising, use proper form, don't overdo it, and listen to your body. Also, ensure you have appropriate gear and equipment, and cool down and stretch after your workout.
